## Icon Composition

The icon consists of:

1. **Background Layer**: Radial gradient from light lavender to purple
2. **Tree Trunk**: Solid brown with subtle highlight
3. **Main Foliage**: Large central circle in sage green
4. **Side Branches**: Two medium circles in teal green
5. **Upper Foliage**: Three circles in soft purple
6. **Top Accents**: Small circles in lavender
7. **Detail Accents**: Tiny circles in light teal and light purple
