# ♟️ Office Politics Mode

## Overview
A specialized coaching mode designed to help leaders navigate complex power dynamics, hidden agendas, and strategic relationships.

## Features
-   **Specialized Prompts**: The AI now understands "Office Politics" context and provides advice on:
    -   Mapping key stakeholders ("Who influences whom?").
    -   Building strategic alliances.
    -   Protecting reputation.
    -   Identifying hidden agendas.
-   **Curated Tips**: The "Quick Tips" carousel now shows specific advice for this mode, such as "Map the players" and "Build alliances".
-   **Focus Area**: The session badge highlights "Focus: Strategy & Influence".

## How to Use
1.  Tap "Start New Session".
2.  Select "Office Politics" from the mode dropdown.
3.  Start recording your thoughts or a conversation draft.
4.  The AI will analyze it through the lens of power dynamics and strategy.
